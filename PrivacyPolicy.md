# Privacy Policy for WaveReader

**Last Updated:** 01/14/2026

## Introduction

The team behind WaveReader is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application WaveReader.

By using WaveReader, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Location Information

**What we collect:**
- Precise location data (GPS coordinates: latitude and longitude)
- Approximate location data (network-based location)

**Why we collect it:**
- To tag your wave measurements with geographic coordinates
- To display your measurement locations on maps
- To provide location-based features within the app

**How it's collected:**
- Only when you grant location permissions
- Only when actively recording and storing wave measurements or using map features
- Not collected in the background when the app is closed

### 2. Sensor Data

**What we collect:**
- Accelerometer readings from your device's motion sensors
- Gyroscope data (if available on your device)

**Why we collect it:**
- To analyze wave motion and calculate wave height, period, and direction
- Essential for the core functionality of the app

**How it's used:**
- Processed locally on your device in real-time
- Converted into wave measurements through spectral analysis
- Stored as measurement data (not raw sensor data)

### 3. Measurement History

**What we collect:**
- Your wave measurements (height, period, direction)
- Date and time of measurements
- Location coordinates where measurements were taken
- Any notes or tags you add to measurements

**Why we collect it:**
- To maintain your measurement history
- To allow you to review past measurements
- To provide data visualization features

**How it's stored:**
- Primarily stored locally on your device
- [IF USING FIREBASE: Optionally synced to secure cloud storage if you create an account]
- [IF NO CLOUD SYNC: Never transmitted off your device]

### 4. Device Information

**What we collect:**
- Device model and manufacturer
- Android version
- App version

**Why we collect it:**
- To ensure compatibility
- To diagnose technical issues
- To improve app performance

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- All measurement data is stored locally on your device
- Uses Android's secure storage mechanisms
- Data remains on your device unless you explicitly export it

### [OPTIONAL - IF USING FIREBASE] Cloud Storage
- If you create an account, data is synced to Firebase Cloud Firestore
- All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS
- Stored in secure Google Cloud Platform data centers
- Protected by Firebase security rules

### Security Measures
- Location data is never sold or shared with third parties
- No advertising networks have access to your data
- Sensor data is processed locally and not transmitted
- Regular security updates and patches

## Your Data Rights

### Access and Control
You have the right to:

- **View Your Data:** Access all your measurements within the app
- **Delete Your Data:**
    - Delete individual measurements in the app
    - Clear all data by clearing app data in Android Settings
    - [IF FIREBASE: Request account deletion by emailing us]
- **Export Your Data:** Export your measurements to CSV or other formats
- **Control Location Access:** Revoke location permissions at any time in Android Settings

### How to Delete Your Data:

**Option 1: Within the App**
1. Open Wave Reader
2. Go to Settings
3. Select "Delete All Data" or delete individual measurements
4. Confirm deletion

**Option 2: Android Settings**
1. Go to Settings > Apps > Wave Reader
2. Select "Storage"
3. Tap "Clear Data"

**Option 3: Uninstall**
- Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data
- [IF FIREBASE: Cloud-synced data requires account deletion request]

**Option 4: Request Account Deletion**
- Email at maciels.business@gmail.com
- Subject: "Data Deletion Request"
- Include your account email
- We will delete your data within 30 days
